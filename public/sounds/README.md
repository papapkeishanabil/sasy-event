# Sound Effects

Place your sound files here:

- `success.mp3` - Play when guest successfully checks in
- `error.mp3` - Play when QR scan fails or guest already checked in

## Recommended sounds

You can use free sounds from:
- Zapsplat.com
- Freesound.org
- Pixabay audio

For a premium event, use subtle, elegant sounds:
- Success: Soft chime or gentle notification
- Error: Subtle beep (not too harsh)
